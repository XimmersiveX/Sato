Assurance against the risk involved with Asbestos and Lead

Sato Environmental Consultants is a well established asbestos / lead sampling, surveying and consultancy business located in Reno, Nevada. We offer commercial asbestos and lead solutions throughout Nevada and California. We are committed to providing safe and reliable abatement services to our clients.

Our team is comprised of trained and certified asbestos / lead abatement professionals who use state-of-the-art equipment to ensure the safe and efficient removal of asbestos and/or lead from commercial properties.
